Pitch any company with a personalized Gamma deck

Give the skill a target company and your offer. It researches the company, generates a tailored Gamma deck that pitches you to them slide by slide, then finds and enriches the exact decision-maker who should receive it.

How it works: the skill plays the role of an account-based sales strategist inside Claude. It researches the target company with a few web searches, maps your offer to the buyer personas most likely to care, finds the right decision-maker through FullEnrich, and enriches their verified email and phone. Then it generates a Gamma deck that names the target company on nearly every slide, so the pitch reads like it was built for that one account. You get the deck link and the contact in a single package, account-based outreach, one target at a time.

Download Skill
1
Connect both MCPs to Claude
The skill needs two MCPs active in your Claude workspace before it can run anything:
  • FullEnrich MCP at https://mcp.fullenrich.com/mcp
  • Gamma MCP, the official Gamma MCP server
Web search is also used to research the target company, and it is built into Claude, so there is nothing extra to set up. New to MCPs? Follow the FullEnrich MCP setup guide, the Gamma one works the same way.
2
Install the skill in Claude
Click Download Skill above to grab the skill .zip file, then install it in Claude:
  • Open Customize in Claude
  • Click Create new skill
  • Click the + button at the top
  • Choose Create Skill
  • Pick Upload a skill and select the .zip you just downloaded
3
Tell Claude the company and your pitch
Activate the skill with a target company and what you are pitching. Examples:
  • "Create a Gamma deck pitching Tool Monsters to stripe.com and find the right decision-maker"
  • "Generate a personalized pitch deck for datadog.com and enrich the VP of Engineering"
  • "Build a deck for hubspot.com showing how our tool helps and give me the CRO's email"
The skill needs the target company, your offer in a sentence or two, and a sense of the buyer. It asks only for whatever is missing.
4
The skill researches the company and maps buyers
The skill runs a few web searches on the target company to build context:
  • What the company does, their key product, the market they serve, their size and stage
  • Recent signals like funding, new execs, product launches, or hiring trends from the careers page
From your offer and that research, it proposes 2 to 3 buyer personas (for example VP Engineering, Head of Platform, or CTO for a DevOps tool) and waits for you to choose which one to find.
5
It finds and enriches the decision-maker
The skill searches FullEnrich for that persona at the target company. If several people match, it shows 2 to 3 candidates so you pick. If none match, it suggests a broader title or an adjacent persona. Once the person is confirmed, it shows the enrichment cost (~11 credits for one contact) and waits for your explicit yes before enriching their verified work email and phone.
6
It generates the deck and delivers the package
With the research in hand, the skill generates a Gamma deck of 6 to 8 slides built around the target company: a hook, their specific pain, your solution, why you, the concrete value for them, and a clear next step. It then delivers the full package:
  • The Gamma deck link, ready to open and refine in the Gamma editor
  • The decision-maker's name, title, verified email and phone, LinkedIn, and career path
Edits to the deck happen directly in Gamma, where you have full control of the layout and wording.
Built-in safeguards: the skill always researches the company before generating a deck, so the slides reference the real account rather than generic claims. It never invents company facts, maps the persona to your offer instead of defaulting to "CEO", waits for your choice of persona, and asks for explicit confirmation on the enrichment cost. It is built for one account at a time, so for mass outreach it points you to the prospecting and outreach skills instead.
What is next? After delivery, the skill offers to draft a personalized intro email that references the deck, find a second champion at the company, push the contact to your CRM with the deck link saved as a note, or run the whole flow again for another company.

Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
Claude says an MCP is not connectedOpen Claude settings and connect the missing MCP (FullEnrich at the URL above, or the Gamma MCP server). The skill resumes on the next message.
Web search finds little about the companyThe domain may be wrong or the company has a thin online footprint. The skill flags this and asks you to confirm the URL or share context you have.
No decision-maker found at the companyThe company may be small or privacy-conscious. The skill suggests a broader title or an adjacent persona from the persona map.
You want 50 decks for 50 companiesThis skill is account-based, one target at a time. For volume, use the prospecting and outreach skills instead.
You want to change the deck wordingThe Gamma URL is the deliverable. Open it in the Gamma editor to adjust layout, text, and visuals directly.
"CREDITS_INSUFFICIENT" on the contactDespite the name, this status means no data was found for that contact, not that you are out of credits. The skill flags this clearly when it surfaces.
