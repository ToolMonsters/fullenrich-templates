Enrich a contact list with emails & phones

Transform a simple list of names into complete outreach-ready profiles with verified emails, phone numbers, and professional context — powered by FullEnrich inside Clay.

Clay
What this template does: Upload a CSV with contact names and companies into Clay. The template runs 4 enrichments in cascade — FullEnrich Work Email, FullEnrich Mobile Phone, Clay Enrich Person, and Clay Enrich Company — to give you verified emails, direct phone numbers, job titles, and company data. Ready for outreach in minutes.

Get Template
1
Upload your contact list
Click Use Template below to copy the Clay table into your workspace. Then upload a CSV file with your contacts. Minimum required columns:
  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Company Name
Optional but recommended (improves match rates):
  • Company Domain
  • LinkedIn URL

Example CSV format:
First Name Last Name Company Name Company Domain LinkedIn URL
John Doe Acme Inc acme.com linkedin.com/in/johndoe
Jane Smith TechCorp techcorp.io
2
Connect your FullEnrich API key
Before running the enrichments, you need to connect your FullEnrich account to Clay:
  • Go to Settings → Integrations in Clay
  • Search for FullEnrich
  • Click Connect and paste your FullEnrich API key
You can find your API key in your FullEnrich dashboard → Settings → API.
3
Run the enrichments
The template includes 4 enrichment columns pre-configured. Click Run on each column to start the cascade:

FullEnrich Work Email

1.5 credits / contact

Finds the verified professional email using name + company + domain + LinkedIn.

FullEnrich Mobile Phone

25 credits / contact

Finds the direct mobile number. Same input logic. Perfect for cold calling.

Clay Enrich Person

0.5 credits / contact

Extracts job title (e.g. "CEO & Cofounder") and enriches the LinkedIn profile.

Clay Enrich Company

0.5 credits / contact

Gets company size, industry, location, and founding year.

4
Wait for enrichments to complete
Enrichments run in parallel. Depending on list size, this takes a few minutes. You can monitor progress directly in Clay — each row shows its enrichment status.
5
Export your enriched list
All enriched data sits in separate columns: email, phone, job title, company size, industry, location, founding year. Export to:
  • CSV — for any tool
  • CRM — push directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.
  • Email sequencer — Instantly, lemlist, Smartlead, etc.
Your list is ready for outreach campaigns.

Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
FullEnrich returns no emailMake sure First Name, Last Name, and Company Name are mapped correctly. Adding Company Domain or LinkedIn URL significantly improves match rates.
Phone enrichment returns emptyMobile numbers are harder to find. Ensure the contact is a real person with a public business profile. Some regions have lower coverage.
Clay Enrich Person shows no job titleThe person may not have a LinkedIn profile or it may be set to private. Try adding their LinkedIn URL manually.
Company data is missingCheck that the Company Name is spelled correctly. Adding the Company Domain helps Clay match the right entity.
